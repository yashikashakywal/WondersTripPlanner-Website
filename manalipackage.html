Explore Manali: 5 Days & 4 Nights

Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Himalayas with our Manali package. Includes guided tours, accommodation, meals, and transfers.

Travel Dates: 1st Dec - 5th Dec 2024
Price: ₹ 25,000 / per person

Detailed Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Manali. Check-in at the hotel. Leisure day to explore the local market.
  • Day 2: Visit Solang Valley for adventure sports like paragliding, skiing, and zorbing. Overnight stay.
  • Day 3: Excursion to Rohtang Pass (subject to weather conditions). Enjoy the snow and scenic views.
  • Day 4: Explore Old Manali, Hadimba Temple, and the Manu Temple.
  • Day 5: Checkout and depart for your onward journey.
